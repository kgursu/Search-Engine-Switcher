Welcome to Switch Search Engine

Two quick steps and you're ready to go.

1
Pin the extension to your toolbar Click the puzzle piece icon (🧩) in the toolbar → find Switch Search Engine → click the pin icon.
2
Allow access to search pages Click the button below, then open the Extensions page and enable Allow access to search page results.
How it works: When you click the toolbar icon on a search results page, your current query is automatically carried over to the next search engine in your list.
